Welcome

The internet is an informal term for the world-wide communication network of computers. It sends information quickly between computers around the world. It has millions of smaller domestic, academic, business, and government networks, which together carry many different kinds of information. The World Wide Web is one of its biggest services. It is used by a few billion people all over the world.
